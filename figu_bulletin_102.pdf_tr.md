Der Begriff der Gewalt und die Bewertung von Gewalt ändert sich im historischen und sozialen Kontext. 确实，有一种值得尊敬（神圣）的愤怒，它在正义与自制中运行，旨在通过“暴力的非暴力”（基于逻辑与理性的行动）来对抗不公正与不道德的行为，从而实现公正与道德。这种“暴力的非暴力”是被动逻辑力量的道路，因为“暴力的非暴力”意味着以其他方式积极实践非暴力，即通过强制或积极的方式行使非暴力。另一方面，“暴力的非暴力”也代表被动的抵抗，其中被动性作为一种力量或被动的力量，在这种意义上，力量、权力和暴力，即使在非暴力和被动的形式下，作为抵抗，都表现为一种暴力的形式，但这种暴力是在“暴力的非暴力”中行使的。然而，这种无暴力的、被动的暴力不应以人类对负面暴力的通常理解来理解，而应仅在一种积极的、令人满意的、和谐的、补偿的、提升的和秩序创建的力量应用中理解，这种力量以被动抵抗的形式通过逻辑方式施加影响。

当一个人或一群人希望以“暴力的非暴力”实现某个目标时，他们首先必须具备耐心，以便逐步实现目标。例如，一个村庄几公里外有一个美丽的观景点，计划供游客使用。虽然没有修建道路，但通往那里的路径崎岖不平，满是荆棘和黑莓丛。村庄社区决定建造一条通往观景点的道路，以便更多人可以在那里休闲。尽管建设过程艰难，但目标依然坚定不移。通过积极的非暴力，逐步清除障碍：谨慎地砍伐树木，排干湿地等。道路建成后，人们可以畅通无阻地行走和驾驶。在建设过程中，必须克服分心、放弃的冲动、冷漠、懒惰、舒适、困难等，以确保集体项目成功。通过克服这些挑战，人们获得能量和力量，用于其他方面的发展。

**（三）教育中的暴力与非暴力**

暴力不仅存在于个人层面，也存在于人际互动中，尤其是在儿童教育中。父母往往期望孩子按照他们的想法行事，忽视了孩子的个性发展。孩子受到斥责、尖叫和体罚，以灌输行为规范。这种暴力是对孩子人性的侵犯，扼杀其发展。正如《FIGU简报》第72期所述：“如果儿童因错误教育或环境影响而受到暴力对待，这种经历往往会伴随他们一生，导致他们反复表现出暴力行为。”

**（四）暴力对社会的影响**

暴力教育在许多家庭中根深蒂固，阻碍了个人发展和进化。这种暴力蔓延到社会，导致毁灭性后果。政治家、企业领袖和宗教领袖提出解决问题的方案，但这些方案往往未能触及根源。如果基于逻辑与理性，可以用“暴力的非暴力”来解决，但在现实世界中，目标往往通过暴力强加，带来巨大附带损害。这种暴力不仅影响个人，也影响企业、社会和地球。

**（五）暴力的循环**

正如《FIGU简报》所述，经历过暴力的人更容易成为施暴者。暴力不仅直接施加于人，通过目睹暴力也会产生深远影响。每个暴力行为都会引发连锁反应，破坏进化进程。

**（六）以和平的暴力推动进化**

通过“暴力的非暴力”，目标的实现需要更长时间，但确保了逐步接近预定目标。每个人在冲突中学习，不同观点、想法和思维模式得以表达。这种基于创造性的自然法则的方法，推动了整体进化，促进了普遍进步。

**（七）传播真理的非暴力方式**

以“暴力的非暴力”传播真理的最佳例证是《FIGU》的“静默革命”。通过研究《FIGU》的文献和网络出版物，人们可以自主探索真理，而非被动接受。这种自我教育过程改变了个人的思想、行为和性格，进而影响周围环境。这种转变无法通过宗教、哲学或政治实现，而是通过个人的内在转变。

我有幸在过去的20多年里，能够在比利（Billy）身边，亲眼目睹他在各种日常情境中的工作和生活。因此，我今天可以说，自1970年代以来，比利仅凭一只手臂，为整个世界及其未来做出了非凡的、甚至超乎常人的身体和精神贡献，正如他一生中所做的那样，这令人惊叹。

Er unterstützt uns auch bei Gartenarbeiten, beim Betonieren, bei der Haushaltarbeit sowie beim Kochen. Tag und Nacht setzt er sich unermüdlich mit Rat, Tat und Kraft für Menschen in aller Welt ein. Dank den wertvollen erweiterten Informationen von Sfath, zusammen mit dem besonders phantastischen Erlebnis an jenem Sonntag bezüglich der Augen von Billy, wird auch für mich persönlich eine sehr wertvolle Erweiterung meines Verständnisses des Begriffes ‹Gott› zu einem unschätzbaren Wert, der nun meinen Weg wie ein Leuchtband durch mein Leben ziehen lässt.

In tiefer Dankbarkeit und Ehrwürdigkeit bedanke ich mich bei Billy für seine immens grossen Leistungen, die er mit ununterbrochener Geduld sein ganzes Leben lang einsetzt, so speziell in bezug auf die einzelnen Menschen, wobei er immer darauf bedacht ist, dass sie ihre innere Ruhe finden, wie er auch um den Weltfrieden auf der Erde bemüht ist und sich dafür einsetzt.

因此，从今以后，人们必须不再背负过去的包袱，即自童年和青年时期以来一直给他们带来悲伤、问题和忧虑并阻止他们找到正确人生道路的一切，这些已使他们陷入不幸和不满之中。现在必须向前迈进，生活在当下，也生活在未来。如此，人必须仅仅过自己的生活，并在自己心中使其生动起来，以便通过一切在当下和未来使他们感到快乐和满足的事物，深深地触动自己。因此，每天他/她都必须为自己留出足够的时间，有意识地自问一些重要问题，即在他/她受挫、不幸和不满的状态下，他/她究竟是如何、什么和谁——以及他/她真正希望成为如何、什么和谁。他/她每天都必须有足够的时间，并且向自己提出这些重要问题，因为他/她必须学会了解自己究竟是如何、什么和谁。人们可以每天自行选择时间提出这些重要问题并对其作出评估，无论是早晨、中午或晚上，还是在一天当中的任何时候，因为当他/她真正有意识地愿意时，总会为此找到时间。每次不必长达一小时，而可以仅仅是五或十、十五或二十分钟，但这会逐渐带给他/她成功、认识，以及缓慢但确实会到来的内心平静与自由。因此，他/她也可以允许自己在平时的空闲时间以及周末和假期中，暂时隐居到静默之中，完全与自己独处，无论是在自己的房间里还是在自由的自然中，一方面是为了向自己提出重要问题，另一方面是为了集中深入地探索自己的内心世界，然后将获得的认知向外渗透到外部世界。这样，他/她可以有意识地改革、重塑和利用自己的行为方式，逐渐在自身中创造幸福和满足。如此，他/她有意识地致力于本质的东西，特别是他/她与自己的关系和他/她自己的生活。从此以后，人现在必须培养自问这些重要问题，因为它们必须成为他/她生活的一部分，因为通过偶尔向自己提出关于自己内心情况、自己是如何、什么和谁以及自己如何处理和理解一切的重要问题，他/她始终知道自己处于何处。如此，他/她也学会理解自己的生活是一件极其珍贵的无价之宝，是一颗他/她可以通过健康的思维和感觉以及新获得的幸福和满足有意识地放射光芒的宝石。在此过程中，他/她必须始终保持清醒——这一点必须不时重复——他/她对自己来说是他/她生命中最重要的人，作为这样的人他/她值得自己总是尽可能地为自己保留休息和时间，向自己提出重要问题并找到正确的答案。因此，也建议他/她在此期间定期进行适合自己的冥想，冥想的形式和内容可以自行决定和设计，但不应使用基于信仰的形式，而应利用与现实相应的形式。

And this is so because he himself/she herself is not a product of belief neither are his/her life and the exis tence, but rather a living human being who leads an effective material life in the existent existence, who, all alone according to his/her own will, forms and directs it through his/her consciousness and his/her thoughts and feelings. He/she must be equally clear and conscious of it, because this cognition and the thereout resulting knowledge contribute, amongst various other things, to the fact that the human being can change his/her life fundamentally to the good and positive. The whole thing done and carried out according to all that as it is described to him/her, belongs to all the effective things which he/she finds in himself/herself and by which he/she can developmentally change to the good, happiness and satisfaction, if he/she asks the necessary important questions to himself/herself and finds the necessary answers from this. These are then the questions which help him/her to the right action and behaviour in his/her outer world and thus also to a new direction in his/her life, whereby he/she leaves behind and dissolves all the miserable and bad things as well as all the burdensome life-problems, which have made his/her life, out of the bad influences from his/her past, to the psychological horror and to the unhappiness and unsatisfaction.
这是因为，他/她本身并非信仰的产物，他/她的生活和存在也不是。相反，他/她是一个活生生的人，在实际存在的现实中过着有效率的物质生活，凭借自己的意志，通过他/她的意识、思想和感情来塑造和引导这一切。他/她必须同样清晰地意识到这一点，因为这种认知以及由此产生的知识，与其他诸多因素一起，有助于他/她从根本上改变自己的生活，向好的和积极的方向发展。如果按照对他/她所描述的那样去行动和实现，这一切都属于他/她在自身内发现的那些有效的事物，通过这些事物，如果他/她向自己提出必要的重要问题，并从中找到必要的答案，就能够在发展过程中转向美好、幸福和满足。这些正是帮助他/她在外部世界中采取正确行动和行为的问题，从而也为他/她的生活带来新的方向，使他/她摆脱并化解所有源自过去不良影响所带来的悲惨和糟糕的事物，以及那些曾使他/她的生活陷入心理恐惧、不幸和不满的沉重生活问题。

But to do all this and to carry it through as well as to achieve all the best, good and positive, he himself/she herself has to consciously take the responsibility and also really bear it and fulfil it, because only therethrough does he/she really overcome his/her wrong, negative and bad thoughts and feelings, through which he/she has tumbled away – powerless to act – up until now, because he/she has simply let himself/ herself go uncontrolled.
然而，若要实现这一切并贯彻始终，以及达成所有最好、良善和积极的目标，他或她自身必须自觉地承担责任，并真正地承担、履行之。因为只有这样，他或她才能真正克服那些错误、消极和不良的思想与情感——正是这些情感和思想，使他或她至今为止无力行动，陷入困境，只因他或她放任自我，不加控制。

It is now for the human being finally the time, in which he/she must open his/her thoughts and feelings for his/her new life, however he/she must also be clear and conscious that all that which he/she finds as an answer through his/her important questions to himself/herself, he/she must realise in a good and positive kind and wise. This however affects everything that he/she perceives in the inner part of his/her outer world as well as in his/her body, in all his/her relationships in and to himself/herself, as well as to the fellow human beings, the nature and its fauna and flora as well as to the whole environment. And he/ she must also be aware that in an unconscious wise, he/she himself/herself has created and drawn every thing to himself/herself, it is therefore not any fellow human beings or the environment that has stamped the unhappiness and the unsatisfaction on him/her. Even if he/she has had a wrong upbringing, in the end it was he/she himself/herself, who has formed himself/herself unhappy and unsatisfied, because he/she has simply let himself/herself defiantly or glumly go, just as he/she also did not make an effort in his/her puberty-time to carry out his/her own education, which would have been extremely important.
现在，人类终于到了这样一个时刻，他/她必须为新生活开启自己的思想和情感，然而，他/她也必须清楚并意识到，所有他/她通过向自己提出重要问题而得到的答案，他/她必须以一种良好、积极的方式和智慧来实现。这涉及到他/她在外在世界的内外部分，以及在他/她的身体，在与他/她自己，以及与他/她的同伴、自然及其动植物，乃至整个环境的所有关系中所感知到的一切。并且，他/她也必须意识到，在不自觉的智慧中，他/她自己已经创造并将一切吸引到自己身边，因此，并不是任何同伴或环境给他/她打上了不幸和不满足的烙印。即使他/她曾经受过错误的教育，但归根结底，是他/她自己塑造了自己不幸和不满足的形象，因为他/她只是任性地或忧郁地放任自己，就像他/她在青春期时也没有努力完成自己的教育，这是极其重要的。

And the fact that this would have been very important, this he/she did not know of course, because that was not explained to him/her through his/her up-bringing in childhood, consequently he/she also could not know what he/she has done and thus brought about harm to himself/herself and thus also included those fellow human beings therein, who were close to him/her and are still close to him/her. Because of his/her self-condemning thinking, which has formed him/her since his/her youth, he/she has become a victim of himself/herself as well as of his/her fellow human beings and of life, and this strengthens his/her own condemnation against himself/herself as well as against his/her fellow human beings, his/her life and destiny, consequently his/her thoughts and feelings with regard to himself/herself and towards the whole environment center around an existence of being a victim. Thus, it is the thoughts and feelings of the human being towards and about himself/herself, his/her life and destiny and his/her fellow human beings, through which he/she is unhappy and unsatisfied in his/her external world and in its inner part, makes his/her wrong ideas, likewise chooses his/her wrong words, commits the wrong actions and shows wrong modes of behaviour.
他/她并不知道这一点的重要性，因为他/她在童年时期的教育中并未被解释过这一点，因而他/她无法知道自己所做的事情，以及因此给自己和周围亲近的人带来的伤害。由于他/她从青年时期就形成的自我谴责思维，他/她成为了自己和周围人以及生活的受害者，这加强了他/她对自己、周围人、生活及命运的自我谴责。因此，他/她的思想和情感围绕着自己作为一个受害者的存在而存在。因此，正是他/她对自己、自己的生活与命运以及周围人的思想和情感，使他/她在外部世界和内心都感到不快乐和不满足，产生错误的想法，选择错误的言辞，做出错误的行为，并表现出错误的行为模式。

And this he/she has already been doing for a very long time, usually since his/her childhood, and with that he/she terrorises himself/herself and his/her environment, into which he/she sowed bad and terrible things a long time ago, which he/she is reaping in the form of his/her unhappiness and unsatisfaction.
他/她这么做已经很久了，通常是从童年时期就开始，这种行为不仅让他/她自己感到恐惧，也让他/她周围的人感到害怕。很久以前，他/她就在这环境中播下了恶劣和可怕的种子，如今这些种子以他/她的不幸和不满的形式结出了果实。

But, exactly this the human being must learn to recognise and understand, which he/she can only do, how ever, by asking important questions to himself/herself in such a wise, in order to thereby recognise his/her damages in the inner part of his/her outer world and to correct them in a good and positive wise. He/she must recognise, realise and understand everything regarding this, then create energies and powers in himself/herself, in his/her thoughts and feelings, which he/she uses and sends out into his/her entire outer world of all his/her impulsations. He/she must take and developmentally change everything to the best and good, which he/she finds weak and bad in the inner part of his/her outer world, in order to then replace it with all the best, good and positive from his/her creational-natural inner world, in order to become happy and satisfied. As such, the human being must be his/her own creator and take and utilise every good energy form and power from his/her creational-natural inner world, in order to manifest it in his/her outer world and its inner part. Thus, on the one hand, he/she gains all the high and positive values from his/her true innermost and creational-natural Wesen, which is his/her effective inner world, and on the other hand, his/her life as such is thereby provided with health, peace, freedom, happiness, harmony and confidence as well as satisfaction. So the whole thing means that his/her life itself heals in every kind and wise, every life-sickness, all want/lack/deficiency and unpeace, every disharmony, depression and heaviness fade away and that all these terrible things are replaced by true happiness, harmony, lightness and satisfaction. Now, if the human being acknowledges all that which is right and rational and decides to do it, and to see himself/herself as a valueful human being, as all things are explained to him/her, then he/she can only win. From now on, he/she must make an upright effort to be his/her own creator in an absolutely conscious wise, as is fundamentally pre-given to him/her by the nature of the Creation in his/her innermost nature, in his/her inner world. And if he/she does this, then he/she frees himself/herself from his/her life-long well-cared-for victim-consciousness and becomes a self-creative, free human being.
然而，人类必须学会认识并理解这一点，而要做到这一点，唯有通过向自己提出深刻的问题，以智慧的方式，从而认识到其外在世界内部所受到的损害，并以积极的方式加以纠正。他/她必须全面认识、理解并领悟这一切，然后在自身，在思想和情感中，创造能量与力量，并将其运用并投射到整个充满所有冲动的外在世界中。他/她必须将内在世界中发现的每一处软弱与不良之处，转化为最佳与美好的状态，并以创造性自然内在世界中所有美好、积极的事物加以替代，从而达到幸福与满足。因此，人类必须成为自身的创造者，汲取并利用其创造性自然内在世界中的每一种正能量形式与力量，将其显化于外在世界及其内部。这样一来，一方面，他/她汲取了源自其最深层真实且创造性自然的"本质"的所有崇高与积极价值——这是他/她有效的内在世界；另一方面，他/她的生活也因此被赋予了健康、和平、自由、幸福、和谐、信心以及满足感。这意味着，他/她的生活本身将以各种方式得到疗愈，所有的生命疾病、匮乏、不安、矛盾、抑郁和沉重都将消散，被真正的幸福、和谐、轻盈和满足所取代。现在，若人类认可所有正确且理性的事物，并决心付诸实践，视自己为有价值的个体，正如一切向他/她所阐释的那样，那么他/她必将收获成功。从此以后，他/她必须以完全自觉的方式，努力成为自己的创造者，这在其内在世界的创世本质中已被预先赋予。若他/她能够做到这一点，即可摆脱长期依赖他人照顾的受害者意识，成为一个自主创造、自由发展的人类个体。

In other words, this means that from now on, he/she must take over, bear and exert his/her own creatorresponsibility of anything and everything, that he/she gets to know in himself/herself by asking the important questions to himself/herself. And he/she must also perceive and implement the creator’s responsibility for everything, so the quality of his/her whole life, as well as what he/she finds in his/her life itself as well, be it with regard to his/her body, his/her thoughts, feelings and the psyche, his/her ideas, require ments and desires as well as concerning his/her relationship to himself/herself, to the fellow human beings, nature and its fauna and flora and the environment as a whole. From now on, the human being has to be ready to take his/her life consciously and lovingly into his/her own hands, to direct it and to make the best possible out of it. And if he/she does so, then he himself/she herself thereby opens a gate to a way that leads him/her out of the powerlessness of dependence on his/her most messed up thoughts and feelings as well as the demolished psyche and into the freedom of happiness and satisfaction. He/she has to build up his/her own creative power, perceive it more and more, transform it into reality and thus create a new, good and positive as well as free, happy and satisfied life, that lets him/her live an all-around fulfilled life in equalisedness, peace, freedom, harmony and love.
换句话说，这意味着从今以后，他/她必须通过向自己提出重要问题，承担并行使他/她所了解的一切的创造者责任。他/她还必须认识到并实施对一切的创造者责任，因此他/她整个生活的质量，以及他/她在生活中所发现的一切，无论是关于他/她的身体、思想、感情和心理，还是关于他/她的想法、要求和欲望，以及他/她与自己、与同胞、与自然及其动植物以及整体环境的关系，都必须得到提升。从今以后，人类必须准备好有意识并充满爱地将自己的生活掌握在自己手中，指导它并尽力做到最好。如果他/她这样做，那么他/她将为自己打开一扇通向自由、幸福和满足之门，摆脱依赖他/她最混乱的思想和情感以及被破坏的心理状态的无能为力。他/她必须建立自己的创造力，逐渐感知它，将其转化为现实，从而创造一个全新、美好、积极、自由、幸福和满足的生活，使他/她能够在平衡、和平、自由、和谐和爱中过着完全充实的生活。

### In the right situation, the human being may also become loud for once
### 在适当的情况下，人偶尔也可以大声一次。

（英文翻译）

Many a human being, in his/her unhappy and unsatisfied life, has again and again experienced situations ever since his/her childhood, which he/she just could not accept and over which he/she has excessively got angry, because he/she just could not come to terms with it. Thus, for whatever reasons, he/she got angry about his/her parents, possible brothers and sisters, about the grandparents and relatives as well as about acquaintances, friends, about teachers and supervisors, because he/she did not dare stand up to them and to vociferously tell them his/her opinion, when it would have been necessary, on the one hand to make his/her point of view clear to them, and on the other hand, in order to be able to stand one's ground. At the same time he/she has never noticed that in reality he/she has harmed himself/herself by his/her anger and his/her knuckling under, and indeed because he/she in truth got angry with himself/herself, precisely because he/she wrapped himself/herself in silence. However, the whole thing has its reason therein, because neither as a child, nor as an adolescent and adult human being, was it conceded to him/her to once vociferously vent his/her anger in a negative case. In the same wise, this has also been extended to positive impulsations, consequently he/she has also not learned to vociferously express himself/herself for something joyful, happy and satisfying, because this was likewise prevented to him/her. So he/she was forced to scale back the behaviour in every respect and to pull himself/herself together, in order to avoid any vociferous expression regarding his/her opinion, that he/she would have so loved to let out of himself/herself. However, everything, all his/her anger and that which he/she was conscious of as opinion and was on the tip of his/her tongue for utterance, he/she has simply swallowed, instead of bringing up the whole (thing) clearly, precisely and vociferously. With this vociferous-bringingup, however, is not meant quarrelling, but simply a loudly voiced distinct, firm and clear explanation of one's own opinion in relation to the mentioned facts, and indeed without anger being involved in doing so. To raise one's own word vociferously and in the right angerless wise, corresponds thus to an urgent necessity, when, with respect to the voice, it cannot be spoken in a normal, but only in a raised voice. Unfortunately, to speak with a loud and therefore raised voice is often unavoidable, because certain human beings only respond to arguments put forward to them, when the facts to be put forth are vociferously made clear to them.
许多人在他们那不幸且不满的生活中，从童年开始，就一次又一次地经历着那些他们无法接受、并因此过度愤怒的情境，因为他们无法与这些情境达成和解。因此，无论出于何种原因，他们对自己的父母、可能的兄弟姐妹、祖父母和亲戚，以及熟人、朋友、老师和上级领导感到愤怒，因为他们不敢在这些情境中站出来，勇敢地表达自己的观点，这样做一方面是为了明确自己的立场，另一方面也是为了能够坚定不移地维护自己的立场。同时，他们从未意识到，实际上，他们的愤怒和屈服只会伤害到自己，这是因为他们本质上是对自己感到愤怒，正是因为他们选择了沉默。然而，这一切都有其原因，因为无论是作为孩子、青少年还是成人，他们在面对负面情况时，都未曾被容许公开地宣泄他们的愤怒。同样地，这也就延伸到了积极的冲动上，因此他们也未曾学会在遇到喜悦、幸福和满足时大声表达，因为这也被他们自己阻止了。因此，他们被迫在各方面压抑自己的行为，克制自己，以避免任何关于自己意见的公开表达，即便他们内心极渴望表达。然而，他们只是简单地吞下了所有的愤怒、那些他们意识到的观点以及那些想要说出口的话，而不是清晰、准确、大声地表达出来。然而，这里所说的“大声表达”并非指争吵，而是指在提到的事实面前，大声、明确、坚定地表达自己的观点，而且不带有愤怒。在必要时，以无愤怒的方式大声表达自己的话语，是在声音无法以正常方式、只能以更高的语调表达时的紧迫需要。不幸的是，大声说话往往不可避免，因为某些人只有在对他们大声阐明事实时，才会对他们提出的论点作出回应。

If this is necessary, then it must be done precisely in this wise, which however does not have to be so in every case, because usually a normal tone of voice can clarify any concern, unless by one side, it is stubbornly switched to defence and uncomprehension. Unfortunately, becoming loud – without quarrelling – is sometimes not to be avoided, when a human being wants make one's voice heard to another who simply switches to stubbornness. Thus, in such a case, the raising of the voice is justified, however only to the point where no dispute arises and no anger takes hold. However, if disputes and anger arise, then it brings forth very bad psychic, psychosomatic and physical consequences, such as blood vessel narrow ing, shrinking of the gums, the formation of gallstones and kidney stones, as well as hyperacidity of the body and stomach as well as stomach ulcers, gastritis, chronic muscle tensions and muscular cramps, right up to articular calcifications, suppurations of the auxiliary and frontal sinuses and thyroid gland damage, etc.
如果这是必要的，那么就必须准确无误地以这种方式进行，但这并非在每种情况下都必须如此，因为通常而言，正常的语气足以澄清任何关切，除非一方固执地转入防御和不理解的状态。不幸的是，当一个人希望向另一个已经转入固执状态的人表达自己的声音时，有时不可避免地——且并非是在争吵的情况下——需要提高音量。因此，在这种情况下，提高声音是被证明合理的，但仅限于不会引发争吵和愤怒的程度。然而，如果争吵和愤怒发生，将会导致非常糟糕的心理、心身及身体后果，如血管狭窄、牙龈萎缩、胆结石和肾结石的形成，以及身体和胃的过度酸性及胃溃疡、胃炎、慢性肌肉紧张和肌肉痉挛，直至关节钙化、辅助和额窦的化脓及甲状腺损伤等。

When the human being, who is unhappy and unsatisfied, indulges in anger, then he/she misuses his/her consciousness-thought-feeling-psyche-based energies and powers by creating in himself/herself resentment, discord, hatred, fury and rage as well as inner misery and inner hardship. And when anger burns in the human being, then it couples with fury and rage, etc., which the body simply does not accept and cannot cope with, consequently the whole thing spreads in the body and acts psychosomatically and ultimately physically affects the organs in a pathological wise. So then, ultimately, this altogether expresses that which prevails in his/her inner – everything that is chronically suppressed, because he/she does not express himself/herself vociferously and disputelessly within a correct frame in order to make clear his/her own opinion to others. His/her entire physical body – and therefore also all his/her internal organs – is a perfect reflection of his/her consciousness-thought-feeling-psyche-based attitude and his/her effectively given condition as well as his/her real behaviour towards his/her life. So the whole thing is that which he/she fabricates and does to himself/herself, so that it is not life itself that burdens him/her with his/her sufferings, his/her misery, his/her hardship and pain as well as his/her unhappiness and unsatisfaction, but solely and exclusively only he himself/she herself, consequently he/she must set right everything and bring it in a right condition himself/herself. He/she may consider and know very clearly that life per se is not difficult and unfair, but that he/she makes it to this all alone through all his/her wrong thoughts and feelings. He himself/she herself makes his/her life to a hard fight and to a hard school, so he/she also has to pay for the consequences of his/her wrong thought- and feeling-modes.
当一个人处于不幸与不满足的境地，并沉溺于愤怒之中时，他/她便滥用了植根于意识、思维、情感与心理的能量与力量，从而在自身内部滋生出怨恨、不和、仇恨、狂暴与盛怒，以及内心的痛苦与困厄。一旦愤怒之火在人体内燃烧，它便与狂暴和盛怒等情绪交织在一起，而这些是身体本质所无法接纳与应对的，最终导致这些负面情绪在整个身心系统中蔓延，触发身心症状，并以病态方式影响各器官功能。因此，这一切实质上揭示了他/她内心深处持续压抑的一切——由于未能在适当的框架内以无争议且有力的方式表达自我，向他人阐明己见。他/她的全身——包括所有内脏器官——完美地映射出其意识、思维、情感与心理的结构状态，以及他/她对生活的实际态度与真实行为。总而言之，这些困扰并非生活本身强加给他/她的苦难、不幸、困苦、疼痛及不满，而是他/她自身一手造就的结果。因此，他/她必须自行纠正一切并将其恢复正常。他/她应深思并明确认识到，生活本身并非艰难不公，而是他/她通过所有错误的思维与情感独自将其塑造成如此。他/她自己将人生转变为一场艰难的斗争与严酷的学堂，故而他/她也必须为其错误思维与情感模式所带来的后果付出代价。

Effectively, life itself is not responsible for the fact that the human being is getting angry and that he/she hides away in himself/herself and does not express his/her opinion in a normal tone or, if necessary, in a loud voice, if the situation requires it. And the fact that he/she prefers to hide himself/herself away and to remain silent, that is why he/she has made his/her life to a drudgery as well as unhappy and unsatisfied.
实际上，生命本身并不因人类感到愤怒、退缩自我、以及未能以正常语调或必要时的大声表达意见而负责。正是这种倾向于隐藏自己、保持沉默的态度，使得他们的生活变得劳苦，不快乐且不满足。

But from now on, he/she has to change that and in this regard integrate himself/herself into the right forms of life by raising his/her voice in a normal or vociferous wise, if necessary, in order to make his/her opinion known and to make his/her own rules and guidelines clear to his/her fellow human beings. Only in this way can he/she break out of his/her own constriction and become free and full of courage, where through also his/her life-courage grows, which in turn shows him/her the way to happiness and satisfaction, which he/she can gladly tread. May the human being always consider, when he/she is unhappy and unsatisfied, that every cell of his/her body uninterruptedly registers very precisely what he/she thinks and perceives in a feeling-based wise about himself/herself and his/her life as well as about the fellow human beings and the environment. There fore, if he/she does not amply stand up for himself/herself, then all the fibres of his/her consciousness and body perceive this and accordingly switch everything to terribleness and ruin. So from now on, he/she has to stand up for himself/herself, for his/her own well-being, for his/her happiness and for his/her satis faction, which means that he/she now has to create these values in himself/herself, so that every cell of his/her body perceives and registers this. And if this happens by his/her own conscious will, then his/her thoughts and feelings as well as his/her consciousness, his/her psyche and the whole body cannot do otherwise than to react in such a way that happiness and satisfaction build up in him/her, which stream through his/her entire cells of his/her body and convey a comprehensive well-being and joy, as well as peace and harmony to him/her and make him/her inwardly free. From that moment, when this begins to manifest itself in him/her, he/she is no longer a loser, but a winner, who henceforth gets his/her life under control, understands how to master it from now on and can freely and openly also say his/her opinion, be it in a normal tone of conversation or in a vociferous, firm and clarifying kind and wise.
